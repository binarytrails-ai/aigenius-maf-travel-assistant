# Australia Visa Policy

## Overview
Australia requires most foreign nationals to obtain a visa or an Electronic Travel Authority (ETA) before entering the country.

## Visa Types

### Electronic Travel Authority (ETA)
- **Eligible Countries**: UK, USA, Canada, Japan, Singapore, and others
- **Duration**: 12 months (multiple entries)
- **Stay Period**: Up to 3 months per visit
- **Processing Time**: Instant to a few days
- **Cost**: Approximately AUD $20
- **Application**: Online only

### Visitor Visa (subclass 600)
- **Eligibility**: Citizens of countries not eligible for ETA
- **Duration**: 3, 6, or 12 months
- **Stay Period**: As granted by immigration officer
- **Processing Time**: 15-30 working days
- **Cost**: Approximately AUD $190-210
- **Application**: Online via ImmiAccount

### Student Visa (subclass 500)
- **Eligibility**: International students enrolled in approved institutions
- **Duration**: Length of course plus 6 months
- **Work Rights**: Limited work hours allowed during semester
- **Processing Time**: 30-60 days
- **Cost**: Approximately AUD $650
- **Application**: Online via ImmiAccount

### Skilled Migration Visa (subclass 189, 190, 491)
- **Eligibility**: Skilled workers in demand occupations
- **Duration**: 5 years (189, 190), 3 years (491)
- **Work Rights**: Unlimited
- **Processing Time**: 60-90 days
- **Cost**: Varies by visa type
- **Application**: Online via SkillSelect

## Entry Requirements
- Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity recommended)
- Completed application form
- Proof of financial capacity
- Health examination (if required)
- Police certificates (if applicable)
- Travel insurance (recommended)

## Conditions
- Must not work (or follow work restrictions)
- Must maintain health insurance
- Must comply with Australian law
- Must not stay beyond visa expiration

## Key Information
- **Currency**: Australian Dollar (AUD)
- **Capital**: Canberra
- **Official Language**: English
- **Climate**: Varies by region (tropical to temperate)
- **Time Zones**: Multiple zones across country
